⚙️ EmailJS Setup

EmailJS lets you send emails directly from the browser — no backend needed. Follow the steps below to connect your free account.

1

Create a free EmailJS account

Go to emailjs.com and sign up for free. The free tier allows 200 emails/month.

2

Add an Email Service

In your EmailJS dashboard → Email Services → connect Gmail, Outlook, or any SMTP provider. Copy the Service ID.

3

Create an Email Template

Go to Email Templates → Create New. Use these variables in your template:

To: {{to_email}}
Subject: {{subject}}
Body: {{message}}

Copy the Template ID.

4

Get your Public Key

Go to Account → General → copy your Public Key.
